Jeremie, Haiti

Jeremie is a coastal city in southwestern Haiti, often referred to as the "City of Poets" due to its rich literary history and its association with many Haitian writers and intellectuals. The city is known for its picturesque beaches, lush green hills, and colonial-era architecture, making it an appealing destination for visitors seeking both natural beauty and cultural heritage.
